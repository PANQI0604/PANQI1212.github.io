COVID-19

What is coronavirus?

The teeny-tiny virus that has troubled the entire world, belongs to a family that generally makes animals sick. But to our misfortune, coronavirus is among the seven viruses that has made the jump to humans. Before the outbreak happened in China in December, 2019, coronavirus was alien to us. Now, it's the only thing that is talked across the globe, all thanks to its fatal nature.

What are the symptoms of COVID-19?

The symptoms of this disease are similar to flu and pneumonia. These include: Dry Cough，Fever，Fatigue，Aches, sore throat, and nasal congestion. COVID-19 is highly contagious and unfortunately, people might transmit the virus even before they show any symptoms.

How does COVID-19 spread?

It spreads through respiratory droplets produced when an infected person coughs or sneezes. Hence, infected people are advised to wear masks. You should also maintain a distance of more than a meter from a person showing these symptoms. Most importantly, one should stay away from large social gatherings

Should everyone buy a mask to protect oneself from COVID-19?

WHO advises wearing a mask only if you show COVID-19 symptoms. Since the outbreak has been declared a pandemic, you should avoid wasting masks as the world might face a shortage soon.
